I have 20 years of experience as a musician and offer lessons in a variety of genres such as rock, pop, blues, jazz and metal. These are suitable for beginners through to more advanced players and contain everything you need to either start learning or improve your abilities. I cover chords, scales, riffs, solos, and music theory, but can help with any techniques that you would like to learn more about.

Lessons are available in Glasgow but I also offer online lessons over video calls.
